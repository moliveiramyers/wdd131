Site Plan: Sunny Swimwear

Mar Myers

Mar Myers — Mar Myers is an online store of swimsuits and other fashion accessories that comes from Brazil.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the online store is to sell swimsuits by exposing our catalog and promotions to customers in Portugal.

Scenarios

  • “I am a swim athlete. I am looking for a swimsuit appropriate for professional swimming. Do you also have them? ”
